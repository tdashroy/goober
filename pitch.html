Why bother

You know this dance already.

Every 4th of July we scatter across four rental houses, share a handful of golf carts, and coordinate the whole thing by phone tag. Goober turns that circus into two taps.

📞

The old way

Call Todd — he passed the cart to Susan. Text Susan — no answer. Call Wendel — he's charging. Meanwhile you're melting on the pier.

🛺

The Goober way

Open the app, see who's around, tap "Get a ride." Somebody grabs it, rolls up, you hop on. The whole family can see it happening, so nobody double-drives.

Same idea, different soul

It borrows the good part of Uber and throws out the rest.

No strangers, no surge pricing, no obligation. Just family, carts, and bragging rights.

The currency of Goober

No fares. Just offers.

Offer whatever you want for a ride — cookies, a favor, or cold hard cash. Goober quietly keeps the tab, and on the last day it settles up on the family "who owes who" board.

EmilyowesTodd🍪 cookies
DrewowesBob🍺 one cold beer
WendelowesSusana big favor
ToddpaidEmily✓ settled
🧾

Two kinds of scorekeeping

The IOU board tracks the promises — the cookies, beers, favors, and occasional five bucks you offered. Tap "paid" when you settle up. Purely for fun and light-hearted guilt.

Goober Points are the real competition — earned by driving, never spent, feeding straight into the leaderboard and the Golden Goober.

Getting everyone on

You're in before your sunscreen dries.

No passwords, no email, no accounts to remember. I set up the group each year and send everyone a link.

Tap your link

I text you a personal Goober link. Tap it — it already knows your name and number from last year.

Confirm it's you

"Joining as Wendel 👍" — check it's right, tweak your name if you want to be "Fireworks Champ" this year.

Start Goobering

You land in the feed. Request a ride, offer to drive, talk trash on the leaderboard. That's it.

Maybe someday

A live map of every cart

If you all love the first version, the dream is a little GPS tag on each cart — so the map shows exactly where every golf cart is, all the time. No more "which house has the good cart?" We'll only build it if year one is a hit.
